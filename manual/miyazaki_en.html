- What's Miyazaki Museum? -

Talking of Anime, it's Miyazaki. Talking of TV, it's Miyazaki.
An small museum that is named from such a reason.
At this shop, the services like the following are prepared for the time being.


The shop
For further details, please refer to this page.

Animédex
You can see the list of anime you've gathered by now in several form of displaying.
In addition, this Animédex data is recorded at the time of catching new anime during the game.

A stroll mode
This is the mode of only walking around the Japanese Islands.
Let's receive TV radio waves at various places.

Relay stations guide
You can see the position of each TV's relay stations on the map
and you can check the channel number and the output.

3D mode
This is the 3D version of "a stroll mode".
You can choose any altitude you want,
and the state of receiving radiowave will change according to it.

If there are no relay stations
This is another version of "a stroll mode".
There are only radio towers which have the output of above 1Kw.
