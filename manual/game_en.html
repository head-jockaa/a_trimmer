Anime Trimmer X

- How to play -

When the round light appears, an anime is televised there.
Let's fish for it with adjusting a channel.
By the way, the red light shows that 2 programs are televised,
the yellow light shows that more than 3 programs are televised.
And then anime is televised on DBS when the sea is rough.
Let's gather all of them to be the anime master.



Z key shifts the mode from "moving mode" to "antenna mode".
Press C key when you go back from "antenna mode" to "moving mode".
There's another 3 modes in the "antenna mode"
and you change them by turns with X key.


Control at the moving mode
Moving the player is operated by arrow key.
You can also accelerate, but its control varies according to the config.
Please refer to the page Configuration.

Z key shifts the mode to "antenna mode".
C key displays the Menu.
X key makes a sleeping like a log.
You can use this "sleep like a log" to set the time forward.

Control at the antenna mode
There's the following 3 modes in this mode, and you change them by turns with C key.
In any case, you go back to "moving mode" with X key.

  • Turning channel

    This is the mode to select a channel number with Z key and arrow key.
    The way of control varies according to the kind of antenna.
    Please refer to Various antennas

  • Turning antenna

    You whirls an antenna around with arrow key.

  • Trolling

    This mode will be added by purchasing at a shop.
    You move the map with arrow key, examining the state of radiowave.
    The display on the screen varies according to the antenna.

    Explanation of the screen
  • moving mode

    The altitude at that point is displayed when the player stops.
    The number, which is sometimes shown at the downright, shows the "rural ratings".

  • Antenna mode

    The altitude at the radio tower is displayed under the buoy. The amount of tower height is added, too.
    When the radiowave is diffracted on the summit, the altitude at that point is displayed as well.
    The stuff like a buoy shows horizontal polarization by horizontal stripes, vertical polarization by vertical stripes.
    The yellow meter shows the reception level of radiowave. When getting interferrence, it turns red by that amount.

    How to make high score
    The following are the 3 conditions that the score of anime you've catched gets high.

  • Televised by few stations or televised few times a week.
  • Received from a small scale radio tower that output is weak.
  • Received at the point which "rural ratings" is high.

    The rural ratings are automatically calculated by the data of televising anime.
    As the number of anime programs in an area is smaller, the score gets higher.
